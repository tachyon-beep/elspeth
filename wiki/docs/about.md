## About This Wiki

This wiki is a navigation layer over the discussion paper. The paper is the canonical source of truth. The wiki makes it findable, linkable, and actionable for different audiences — breaking the paper's linear structure into cross-referenced pages organised by topic, threat category, and audience.

The wiki does not add new analysis or extend the paper's claims. Where the wiki summarises or restructures content, the original paper text governs in any discrepancy.

## About the Paper

This is a **discussion paper** — an issue-spotting and vocabulary-building contribution grounded in observed patterns from compliance-constrained agentic development. It is not a standard, policy document, or normative guidance.

The paper makes three kinds of claims — observed patterns, analytical inferences, and hypotheses for community validation — and distinguishes them explicitly. Its taxonomy, threat model, and recommendations are presented for refinement, not adoption as-is.

Community input is welcomed. The paper includes explicit falsifiability criteria: the ACF taxonomy would be weakened or invalidated by evidence that agents do not systematically produce defensive anti-patterns in high-assurance contexts, that standard review processes reliably catch semantic trust boundary violations, or that the failure modes are artefacts of a specific model generation.
